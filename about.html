About Nicholas Onguto

Driving Impact Through Research & Innovation

Nicholas Onguto, MSc, MPH, is a consultant and researcher with 10+ years of experience in public health, climate resilience, WASH, and MEAL. He specializes in data science, statistical analysis, and GIS to inform policies, optimize interventions, and enhance accountability in humanitarian and development sectors across Africa, the Middle East, and South Asia.

Expertise

  • Data Science & Analytics – Predictive modeling, impact assessments.
  • Public Health & Epidemiology – Disease surveillance, health systems evaluations.
  • Climate Resilience & WASH – Risk modeling, environmental health sol
  • MEAL & GIS – Advanced M&E, spatial analytics for development.
  • Policy & Program Development – Research-driven decision-making.
